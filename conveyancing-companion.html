The Problem

Buying your first home is exciting—until the conveyancing process begins. Suddenly you're drowning in legal documents filled with unfamiliar terminology, unsure what each stage involves, and uncertain whether things are progressing normally or if something's gone wrong.

Most first-time buyers rely entirely on their solicitor for guidance, but solicitors are busy professionals handling dozens of cases. Questions go unanswered, documents arrive without explanation, and the whole process feels opaque and anxiety-inducing.

The Solution

Conveyancing Companion puts clarity back in the hands of buyers. It's an AI-powered web app that guides you through every stage of the UK conveyancing process, explains legal documents in plain English, and helps you understand what's happening and why—without replacing your solicitor's professional advice.

Key Features

🗺

Journey Guide

A visual 7-stage walkthrough of the entire conveyancing process, from offer accepted to completion. Know exactly where you are, what's coming next, and what to expect at each stage.

📄

Document Decoder

Upload or photograph legal documents and receive AI-powered plain-English explanations. Understand what you're signing without needing a law degree.

📚

Legal Glossary

A searchable database of conveyancing terminology. Look up terms like "restrictive covenant" or "indemnity insurance" and get clear, jargon-free definitions.

📱

Mobile App

Available on iOS and Android, Conveyancing Companion works on any device. Download from the App Store or Google Play for quick access wherever you are.

Built With the 45black Methodology

Conveyancing Companion demonstrates the 45black approach in action: understanding the system (how conveyancing actually works), structuring the knowledge (legal processes and terminology), mapping workflows (the 7-stage journey), and only then applying AI where it genuinely helps (document analysis).

The result is an AI tool that augments human understanding rather than replacing professional advice—keeping the solicitor in the loop while empowering buyers with clarity.
